seascape models

Welcome to the page of the Seascape Models group at Griffith University, led by Dr Chris Brown. We help data tell stories about more effective environmental decisions. We do this by bringing ecological complexity to the planning tools used to inform decisions.

We're excited to be starting a major project on mapping the health of coastal wetlands globally.
