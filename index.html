Welcome to Chris Brown's Seascape Models page

Research

I work on the conservation of marine ecosystems and sustainable management of fisheries. My work brings ecological complexity to the planning tools used to inform decision making.

I am also interested in the impacts of climate change to marine ecosystems. With a team of others, we have been synthesising the global impacts of climate change to marine ecosystems and predicting the implications of future change.

If you are interested in doing a PhD on conservation, fisheries or seascape modelling, get in touch.

Models, data, programming

My research applies both mathematical models and statistical analysis.

I specialise in integrating ecologically complex models with tools for informing decision makers. I also work with large databases of the marine environment.

I use the R programming language and teach R to others. My teaching resources are on this site and are open-access.
