Hsien-Yung Lin

A PhD candidate at the Centre of Excellence for Environmental Decisions, Hsien-Yung is interested in the conservation of diadromous fish. Their different habitat requirements during the different stages of their life history make them vulnerable to a range of different stressors and many migratory fish are under heavy harvesting pressure. Hsien-Yung's research will include species distribution modeling specific to different life history stages, population dynamics modeling, conservation planning and land-sea connectivity for diadromous fish.
